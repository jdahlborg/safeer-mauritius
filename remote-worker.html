Tax Advantages

Tax Benefits for Remote Workers

As a Mauritius tax resident, your globally earned income benefits from one of the world's most attractive regimes.

15%

Flat Personal Income Tax

Once you achieve tax residency (183+ days/year in Mauritius), your worldwide income is subject to a flat 15% tax — no progressive brackets, no surprises.

0%

Capital Gains & Dividends

No capital gains tax and no withholding tax on dividends. If you hold investments, ETFs, or crypto, Mauritius is one of the most efficient places to manage them legally.

45+

Double Tax Treaties

Mauritius has treaties with 45+ countries including most of Europe, India, and the Middle East — protecting you from being taxed twice on the same income.

Important: Tax Residency vs. Domicile

Tax residency in Mauritius requires 183+ days present in the country per tax year. You must also consider your tax obligations in your home country — particularly for those from jurisdictions with exit taxes or worldwide income taxation rules. We connect you with qualified tax advisors who specialise in cross-border situations for remote workers. Tax planning should be done before you move, not after.
