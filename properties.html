Buying in Mauritius

Property Ownership Schemes

Foreigners can legally purchase property in Mauritius through government-approved schemes

PDS — Property Development Scheme

The most common scheme for foreigners. Mixed residential developments with shared amenities. Minimum purchase of USD 375,000 qualifies for a Permanent Residence Permit.

  • ✓ Open to all foreign nationals
  • ✓ Freehold ownership
  • ✓ Permanent Residence at USD 375K+

IRS — Integrated Resort Scheme

Large luxury resort-style developments combining residential units with hotel amenities — spa, golf, beach club. Premium lifestyle with full services included.

  • ✓ Resort-class amenities included
  • ✓ Rental pool options available
  • ✓ Full permanent residence rights

RES — Real Estate Scheme

Smaller-scale residential developments on plots under 10 hectares. Offers more flexibility in size and location. No minimum investment threshold.

  • ✓ No minimum price requirement
  • ✓ Smaller, more intimate developments
  • ✓ Freehold title
