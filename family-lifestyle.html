Education

International Schools Overview

Mauritius has a growing number of excellent international schools. We help you research, visit, and enrol.

Northfields International High School

Grand Baie, North Mauritius

British

A highly regarded British curriculum school offering IGCSE and A-Level qualifications. Popular with English-speaking expatriate families and known for strong academic outcomes and a welcoming international community.

Ages 3–18 IGCSE & A-Levels English medium

Westcoast International School

Flic en Flac, West Mauritius

British

A smaller, community-oriented school on the west coast offering British Cambridge curriculum. Excellent for families living in the Tamarin, Black River, and Flic en Flac areas.

Ages 3–16 Cambridge curriculum English medium

École du Nord

Grand Baie, North Mauritius

French

The leading French-curriculum school in Mauritius, offering the French Baccalauréat. Ideal for French-speaking families or those seeking continuity with the French education system.

Ages 3–18 French Bac French medium

International Preparatory School (IPS)

Moka, Central Mauritius

IB

An IB-accredited school offering the International Baccalaureate curriculum. Excellent for globally mobile families who want internationally recognised qualifications accepted by universities worldwide.

Ages 3–18 IB PYP / MYP / DP English medium

We assist with school research, arrange tours, guide you through the admissions process, and advise on the best school for your child's age, curriculum preference, and location. School places can fill up — we recommend starting the search 3–6 months before your move.

Health & Wellbeing

Healthcare in Mauritius

Mauritius has both an excellent public health system and a growing private sector with world-class facilities.

Public Healthcare

Mauritius provides free public healthcare to all legal residents. The public system includes district hospitals, regional hospitals, and specialised centres. While facilities are generally good, expats often supplement with private health insurance for faster access and specialist care.

✓

Free for All Residents

All legal residents are entitled to free public healthcare services including GP visits, specialist referrals, and hospitalisation.

✓

Dr Jeetoo Hospital & SSRN Hospital

Two main public hospitals in Port Louis and Pamplemousses with comprehensive services including cardiology, oncology, and surgery.

✓

Emergency Services

SAMU (emergency medical service) operates 24/7 across the island with well-equipped ambulances and trained paramedics.

Private Healthcare

Mauritius's private hospitals offer international-standard care with shorter waiting times, English and French-speaking staff, and modern diagnostic equipment. Most expats take out international health insurance.

Apollo Bramwell Hospital

Moka, Central Mauritius

The island's most prestigious private hospital, part of the international Apollo group. Offers comprehensive specialist services including cardiology, orthopaedics, oncology, and a dedicated maternity unit.

Clinique Darné

Floréal, near Port Louis

A long-established private clinic with a strong reputation for surgery, maternity, and specialist consultations. Widely used by the expat community and Mauritian private patients.

Your Social Life

Expat Community & Lifestyle

Mauritius has a warm, welcoming expat community. You'll never feel alone on this island.

🏄

Water Sports

World-class surfing at Tamarin, kite-surfing at Le Morne, diving, snorkelling, deep-sea fishing, and sailing year-round.

🍽️

Dining & Cuisine

A melting pot of Creole, Indian, Chinese, French, and international cuisine. Grand Baie and Port Louis have exceptional restaurant scenes.

⛳

Sports & Fitness

Championship golf courses, tennis clubs, yoga studios, padel courts, hiking trails in Black River Gorges, and a thriving running community.

🤝

Social Clubs

Active expat social groups, international women's clubs, business networking organisations, and regular community events throughout the year.
