Your Options

Permit Types Explained

Mauritius offers several residency pathways. We assess your profile and recommend the best fit.

Premium Visa

Popular

Ideal for remote workers, freelancers, and retirees who wish to live in Mauritius while earning income from abroad.

  • Valid for 1 year, renewable annually
  • Minimum income: USD 1,500/month
  • No work permit required for remote income
  • Processed in 2–4 weeks

Occupation Permit

For professionals employed locally or self-employed individuals operating a Mauritius-based business.

  • Valid for 3 years, renewable
  • Minimum salary: MUR 30,000/month (professionals)
  • Covers professionals & self-employed
  • Processed in 4–6 weeks

Retired Non-Citizen Permit

Designed for retirees aged 50 and above who wish to enjoy Mauritius's exceptional quality of life.

  • Minimum age: 50 years
  • Transfer USD 1,500/month to Mauritius bank
  • Valid 3 years, renewable
  • No active employment allowed

Investor Permit

For entrepreneurs and investors establishing or acquiring a business in Mauritius.

  • Minimum investment: USD 50,000
  • Valid 3 years, renewable
  • Includes spouse & dependants
  • Path to Permanent Residency
Step by Step

The Application Process

We guide you through every stage — from initial assessment to collecting your permit.

1

Free Profile Assessment

We review your nationality, income sources, employment situation, and goals to identify the ideal permit pathway — and flag any potential issues early.

2

Document Collection & Preparation

We provide a tailored checklist and guide you through preparing every document — certified translations, apostilles, bank statements, and more — to meet Mauritius EDB standards.

3

Application Submission

We submit your complete application to the Economic Development Board (EDB) or Passport and Immigration Office on your behalf — correctly and on time.

4

Follow-Up & Communication

We monitor your application's progress, respond to any requests for additional information, and keep you updated throughout — no black holes, no guessing.

5

Permit Collection & Onboarding

Once approved, we accompany you through the collection process and brief you on your rights, obligations, and renewal timeline — so you're fully set up from day one.

Be Prepared

Documents Checklist

Most applications require these documents. Exact requirements vary by permit type — we'll give you a tailored list.

ID Identity Documents

  • Valid passport (min. 18 months validity)
  • Birth certificate (certified & apostilled)
  • Marriage certificate (if applicable)
  • Police clearance certificate

$ Financial Documents

  • 3–6 months bank statements
  • Proof of income or pension
  • Employment contract or client contracts
  • Tax returns (last 2 years)

+ Supporting Documents

  • Proof of accommodation in Mauritius
  • Medical certificate / health clearance
  • CV / professional qualifications
  • Business plan (for Investor Permit)
