Property Purchase

Buying Property as a Foreigner

Mauritius has specific schemes that allow foreigners to purchase property. We connect you with licensed advisors to navigate them.

PDS

Property Development Scheme

The main scheme for buying standalone villas, townhouses, or penthouses in integrated resort developments.

  • Minimum purchase: USD 375,000
  • Automatic Permanent Residence Permit
  • Freehold title deeds
  • No restrictions on rental income
IRS

Integrated Resort Scheme

The original scheme for high-end resort-integrated properties. Largely superseded by PDS but certain legacy developments still operate under IRS.

  • Luxury resort-integrated properties
  • Minimum purchase: USD 375,000
  • Permanent residency included
  • Full ownership rights
RES

Real Estate Scheme

Smaller-scale residential developments designed for foreigners. More affordable entry point but without automatic residency qualification.

  • Typically smaller plots / apartments
  • No minimum purchase price
  • No automatic residency
  • Good value for money
