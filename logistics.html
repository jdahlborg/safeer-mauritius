Our Scope

What We Coordinate

Moving internationally involves more than packing boxes. We handle the full picture.

International Removals

We work with vetted international removal companies to get your belongings from door to door — by sea freight (FCL or LCL) or air freight, depending on your timeline and budget.

  • Pre-move survey & packing estimate
  • Professional packing service available
  • Sea freight: 3–6 weeks; Air freight: 5–10 days
  • Marine insurance coordination
  • Delivery & unpacking at your new home

Customs Advisory & Clearance

Mauritius Customs has specific rules about what can be imported duty-free and what attracts duties. We guide you through the process and connect you with a licensed customs broker.

  • Pre-departure import declaration guidance
  • Duty-free entitlement assessment
  • Licensed customs broker referral
  • Port clearance coordination
  • On-site customs inspection support

Vehicle Import Assistance

Bringing your car to Mauritius is possible but requires navigating import duties, inspection certificates, and left-hand drive conversion (Mauritius drives on the left). We guide every step.

  • Import duty calculation (typically 15–100%)
  • Vehicle age restrictions guidance (max 3 years for most)
  • RoRo or container shipping coordination
  • NTA inspection & registration support
  • Local car purchase alternatives if needed

Pet Relocation Support

Mauritius is a rabies-free island with strict biosecurity rules. Bringing pets requires careful planning months in advance. We guide you through the entire process.

  • Rabies vaccination & titre test timeline
  • Government import permit application
  • Approved airline routing guidance
  • Quarantine facility arrangements
  • Accredited vet referrals for certificates
Know Before You Ship

Mauritius Customs Rules

Understanding what you can import duty-free — and what you can't — saves money and delays.

Duty-Free Allowances for New Residents

🏠

Household Effects

Used personal and household goods imported within 6 months of taking up residence may qualify for full exemption. Items must have been owned and used for at least 6 months prior to import.

👗

Personal Effects

Clothing, personal accessories, and jewellery for personal use are generally admitted duty-free. Commercial quantities are excluded.

💻

Electronics & Work Equipment

Personal computers, tablets, and professional equipment owned prior to arrival generally qualify for duty exemption when declared as personal effects.

Prohibited & Restricted Items

✗

Firearms & Weapons

Strictly prohibited without specific import permits (rarely granted).

✗

Drugs & Narcotics

Strictly prohibited. Severe criminal penalties apply.

⚠

Food Products

Many food items require phytosanitary certificates. Meat, dairy, and fresh produce face restrictions.

⚠

Plants & Soil

Import permits required. Biosecurity inspections may apply to protect the island's ecosystem.

⚠

New Goods & Commercial Quantities

New items and quantities exceeding personal use are subject to VAT (15%) and import duties.

Bringing Your Pet

Pet Relocation to Mauritius

Mauritius is rabies-free — keeping it that way means strict import controls. Start planning at least 6 months before you move.

🐕

Eligible Pets

Dogs and cats from approved countries. Animals must be microchipped, vaccinated, and meet Mauritius biosecurity requirements.

💉

Vaccination Requirements

Rabies vaccination followed by an approved titre test (blood test) to confirm immunity. Test must be done at an EU-approved laboratory. Allow 90+ days after titre test before travel.

🏥

Quarantine

Most pets undergo a 30-day quarantine at an approved Mauritius facility upon arrival. We arrange the best facilities and ensure your pet is comfortable throughout.

Approved Airlines: Not all airlines carry pets as cargo to Mauritius. We advise on approved carriers and routing options from your origin country to ensure a smooth, compliant journey for your pet.
